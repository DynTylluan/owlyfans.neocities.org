Hi there, this website was made for the August 2018 HyperJam, the theme for the first HyperJam was Alternate History. For my theme I picked the idea of what if there was a cult in the mid-1990s that were on an island.

I was impart inspired by the computer game Dusk with the cult theme, the television show Father Ted with the idea of an offshore island, and I also had inspiration from the website CROWCULT for the idea of a website about a cult. All of the events, and people in this project are a work of my own fiction, and are no wayy real.

Despite that this project was made for HyperJam, I never sumited it, due to a number of events going on in the community at the time.
